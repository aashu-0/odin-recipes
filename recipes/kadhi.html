Kadhi Pakora

A classic North Indian dish, Kadhi Pakora is a flavorful and comforting yogurt-based curry with crispy fried gram flour dumplings (pakora) served in a tangy and spicy sauce.

Ingredients

  • For Pakora

  • besan
  • baking soda
  • salt
  • lukewarm water
  • oil

  • For kadhi

  • oil
  • onion
  • green chilies
  • ginger
  • turmeric powder
  • red chili powder
  • garan masala
  • salt

Steps

  1. Prepare the yogurt mixture
  2. Prepare the spice mixture
  3. Simmer the kadhi
  4. Prepare th pakoras
  5. Fry the pakoras
  6. Serve
