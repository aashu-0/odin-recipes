Uthappam

Uthappam, also known as Uttapam, is a popular South Indian breakfast dish originating from the Tamil cuisine. It is a thick, savory pancake made with a fermented rice and lentil batter, typically topped with onions, herbs, and spices.

Ingredients

  • Rice Flour
  • Urad Dal
  • Salt
  • Water
  • Green Chilies
  • Coriander Leaves
  • Onions
  • Tamatoes
  • bell pepper

Steps

  1. Soak rice and lentils separately for 4-5 hours.
  2. Grind the rice and lentils into a smooth batter with water and salt.
  3. Ferment the batter overnight or until it rises and becomes light and airy.
  4. Heat a non-stick pan or dosa tawa over medium heat.
  5. Pour a ladleful of batter onto the pan and spread it evenly.
  6. Top with desired ingredients and drizzle with oil.
  7. Cook until the base is crispy and the top is cooked through.
